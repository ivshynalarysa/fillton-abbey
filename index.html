About Us

Hello!
I am the heart of “Filton Abbey Woof”, an establishment where dogs are groomed with patience, skill and genuine care.
The grooming salon is designed to make your pet not only look flawless, but also feel calm, cozy and loved.
I believe that beauty begins with care - that's why:

  • We use only natural and gentle products
  • We work without stress - with adaptation for anxious dogs
  • We offer an individual approach to each pet
  • I do not deal with anal glands, as this procedure is best performed by veterinary specialists

I have a college degree in dog grooming in which I got a distinction grade. Dog owners are my clients, whom I sincerely love.

Filton Abbey Woof is not just a grooming salon. This is a place where dogs feel comfortable, and their owners are sure that they are in the right hands.
